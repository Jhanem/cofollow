About

What is CoFollow?

CoFollow is a web-based support system designed specifically for students of Liceo de Cagayan, recognizing the critical importance of information for their academic journey.

Our Mission

Acknowledging the school's heavy reliance on the internet, CoFollow aims to provide comprehensive support tailored to the unique needs of Liceo de Cagayan students.

Our Vision

To be the primary online support system for Liceo de Cagayan students, empowering them to achieve academic excellence by providing centralized access to valuable resources, fostering a supportive online community , and promoting a culture of continuous learning.

Our Services

Event Calendar

Provides students with the opportunity to actively participate in and be involved with forthcoming events, fostering their engagement and interaction within the community.

Clubs

Clubs provide an avenue for students to delve into the diverse array of extracurricular activities available at Liceo de Cagayan University.

SMS

Liceo students receive event notifications via SMS for timely updates on upcoming activities and engagements.
